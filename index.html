2021-2022 MERIT TBS Executive Board

19 August, 2021

What you need to know


  • 1.   For those interested in being members of the board of directors please send an e-mail to the club's mail with the subject line "application to ....” specifying the role you are applying for.
  • 2.   Each candidate will receive an email with the application form. Please fill it carefully.
  • 3.   The selected forms will receive an email confimring that they passed to the next step. Afterwards they will need to post a video of max 3 min on our facebook group.
  • 4.   A General Assembly will be held to give a chance for the candidates to address the members with their speeches and plans. A voting process will take place afterwards to elect the new Exectuve Board members.
  • NB:   Please Read the Rules and the Schedule below

RULES


  • 1

    It is allowed to apply in more than one form but you can occupy one position only.


  • 2

    Any member (board member or active member) has the right to apply for the president position.


  • 3

    Resignations of any kind from board members will not be accepted for the duration of the mandate
